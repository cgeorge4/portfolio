Balloon Museum Inspo

Realistic Eye

SZA
Created July 2024, I took inspiration from my experience at the Balloon Museum that came to visit Atlanta. I loved how during the VR experience the animations took the user on a journey and I wanted to create that on a smaller scale with what I had available to me. The big things I’ve learned from working on this project was 1. Lighting is a necessary evil and 2. Camera work is very tricky. This was my first project working more in depth with lighting that, for me, was the most challenging part of this project especially for the opening of the blue room. If I were to revisit it I would close the room off like I did the other rooms rather than leaving it open causing the lighting I did add to disperse to other places it’s not intended to. The next thing that was pretty tricky for me was the actual camera work, with this being the first time I was moving the camera more aggressively in a project which caused me to have to think differently on how I needed the objects to look and not only how I wanted them to look since the camera was consistently moving in one general direction. Created 2024, while working on a team project in TAP (Technologies Ambassadors Program) our team made a handful of 3D projects and one of mine from the group was this realistic eye. The concept was to show how detailed blender can get while using the same techniques we used to build our model for our workshop (https://github.com/TAP-GGC/QuantumQuirks.git). Created in December of 2022 inspired by Sza’s album , SOS.
