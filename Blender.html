Realistic Eye

Created 2024, while working on a team project in TAP (Technologies Ambassadors Program) our team made a handful of 3D projects and one of mine from the group was this realistic eye. The concept was to show how detailed blender can get while using the same techniques we used to build our model for our workshop (https://github.com/TAP-GGC/QuantumQuirks.git).
