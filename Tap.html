Technology Ambassador Program

Technology Ambassadors Program, or TAP, is a course I took in my time at Georgia Gwinnett College. In this course my team and I created workshops for lower level classes, went to outreach events like the Atlanta Science Festival and attended ACMSE at Kennesaw State University, in this workshop we taught students how to create models in Blender and implement them into the Augmented Reality program, Adobe Aero, where they would be able to engage with their objects in the real world.
